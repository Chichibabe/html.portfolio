Best movie according to Richelle

My top 3 movies / show of all time.

Emily in Paris

What I love in this show is, Emily's journey navigating cultural differences and her career adds relatable touch for viewers. And I love how positive she really is in life.

Maleficent

I love how Maleficent reimagines the villain as a complex, relatable character. The emotional journey, stunning visuals, and Maleficent’s bond with Aurora make it a beautiful story of love and redemption.

Old Guard

The Old Guard (both the movie and the comic) is captivating because of its compelling mix of immortality, action, and humanity. What I love the most is how it delves into the emotional toll of immortality—how the characters grapple with loss, loneliness, and the weight of centuries of experiences.

Charlize Theron as Andy stands out for her layered portrayal of a weary leader trying to keep her team together. The deep bond among the immortal characters, especially Joe and Nicky’s heartwarming relationship, adds depth and emotional resonance to the action-packed storyline.

Also, the fight choreography is incredible—fluid and brutal, showing the team’s centuries of battle-hardened expertise. Plus, the twist of a modern betrayal and the introduction of a new immortal like Nile brings a fresh perspective into their timeless existence.
